Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2025

At Midnight Library, we respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when voluntarily submitted through our story submission form. This may include:

  • Your name

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Review and consider your story for publication on the website
  • Contact you if we have questions about your submission
  • Respond to removal or takedown requests

3. How We Store and Share Information

All submitted information is collected through tally.so and sent to a google sheet using make.com. We do not share your personal information with third parties.

4. Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for users aged 13 and up. If you are under 13, you may not use the submission form or send personal information. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that we have received information from someone under 13, we will delete it promptly.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics tools, or advertising services at this time.

6. Your Rights

If you have submitted a story or personal information and would like it removed, please contact us at midnight.sharing.official@gmail.com. You may request deletion of your story or associated data at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, email us at midnight.sharing.official@gmail.com.
