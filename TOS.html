Community Guidelines

Last updated: December 1, 2025

Welcome to Midnight Library, a platform for discovering and sharing original writing.

1. Content Ownership

All stories submitted remain the property of their original authors. By submitting a story, you grant Midnight Library a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license to display, promote, and archive the content on this website. You can request removal of your story at any time by contacting us.

2. User Submissions

By entering a submission, you confirm that:

  • You are the original author of the piece.
  • You are the original owner of the thumbnail if you have submitted one.
  • You are not infringing on the copyright of any other person or entity.
  • The content does not contain hate speech, threats, harassment, or sexually explicit material.

3. Audience and Age Requirements

This website is designed for users aged 13 and up. If you are under the age of 13, you may not submit stories or provide any personal information through this site. If you are between 13 and 18 years old, you must have permission from a parent or legal guardian to submit content.

4. Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for details on what information we collect and how it is used.

5. Moderation and Content Removal

All submissions are reviewed before being published. Midnight Library reserves the right to reject or remove any content at its sole discretion, especially if it violates these terms or community guidelines.

6. Limitation of Liability

Midnight Library is not liable for any content submitted by users, nor for any harm or damages arising from viewing or reading stories hosted on this site. All content is submitted "as is."

7. Takedown Requests

If you believe your work has been used without your permission, or you wish for your story or artwork (for a thumbnail) to be removed, please email us at midnight.sharing.official@gmail.com.

8. Changes to Terms

We may update these Guidelines from time to time. If we do, we will post the revised version on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
